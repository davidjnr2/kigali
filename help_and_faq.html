How do I register on Remote Academy?

*To register on Remote Academy, it's easy! Once on the homepage of the site, simply click on "Sign Up", in the top right. A pop-up opens, which allows you to register, either in one click via your Facebook or Google account, or via a form to fill. If the pop-up does not appear, be sure to disable AdBlock or any strict plug-in preventing it from being opened.
